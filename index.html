Maarif Schools of Sarajevo

Maarif Schools of Sarajevo consists out of the kindergarten, primary and secondary school,
based on Curriculum of the Sarajevo Canton in Bosnian, English and Turkish language, with the most up-to-date equipment and teaching aids. Students also have the support

Courses We Offer

Here at Maarif Schools we offer three programs

Cambridge

The Cambridge framework provides curricula for the key subjects of English, Mathematics and Science. Other subjects are based upon the English National Curriculum. They include Art, Geography, History, ICT (Computers), Music, Physical Education, and Personal and Social Education.

IB

This is the International Baccalaureate (IB). IB programmes aim to provide an education that enables students to make sense of the complexities of the world around them, as well as equipping them with the skills and dispositions needed for taking responsible action for the future.

Canton program

The cantonal program is an excellent one offered by the ministry of education, and it teaches in Bosnian.
